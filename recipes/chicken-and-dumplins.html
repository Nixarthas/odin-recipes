Chicken and Dumplins

This is a great cold weather food!

    Ingredents
  • Chicken
  • Biscuts in a can (x4)

    Steps
  1. Get a big boiler and fill with water
  2. Once the water boils, add the chicken
  3. Cook the chicken until it is completely white
  4. Remove the chicken and shred it
  5. Add it back and tear the biscuts and add them to the water
  6. Season to taste
